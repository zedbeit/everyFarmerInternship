Coding Challenge. Action Game: Write a simple program that allows to players to fight each other. Each player have their separate weapons and each weapon has its level of damage strength it can inflict on its opponent. Give each player a default life span of 10 and the more the player get hits the more the life span reduces. Remember each weapon has different rate of damage it can inflict on its opponent depending on the weapons damage strength. So the bigger the weapon, the bigger the rate at which the life span reduces.  Note: You can make the computer the player 1 and make me the player as Player 2.
